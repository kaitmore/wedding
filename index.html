Event details

Our wedding and celebration will be mainly outdoors on grass at a beautiful home we are renting in historic Chestertown. So - please choose shoes that won’t sink in the grass and please know that we will have to have guests use portable outdoor restrooms since the plumbing in the house isn’t set up for events of our size.

Expect delicious food, beautiful wine, cannabis, beer and warm company.

Musician friends - bring your instruments if you can! We want to celebrate by making music with you. We will have string quartets, trio sonatas and duos to read and perhaps a little improv but bring your favorite piece along too! There is a piano inside the house.

While so many of us are apart in solidarity for each other’s safety - we really look forward to bringing everyone together in an intimate way at a time when restrictions on gathering should be eased. Regardless the party will be under 50 people but of course if you prefer to celebrate with us virtually for health reasons we can figure out a solution!

Much love,
Alexa and Kait
